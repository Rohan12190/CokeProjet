Our Timeline

1886

The First Glass

Dr. John S. Pemberton, a pharmacist in Atlanta, creates the first Coca-Cola syrup. It's first sold at Jacobs' Pharmacy for five cents a glass.

1899

The First Bottle

The rights to bottle Coca-Cola are sold for $1, marking the beginning of the worldwide bottling system that puts Coke within arm's reach of desire.
